Hey, I'm Milinda

I am a Computer Science PhD student at Indiana University. I'm working as a research assistant in Data to Insight Center under the supervision of Prof. Beth Plale. I am working on research related to moving scientific computations to cloud and currently working with Windows Azure.

I worked at WSO2 Inc. from May 2008 to August 2011 and lead the WSO2 Business Process Server team. I was the founding developer of WSO2 Business Process Server and also involved in developing WSO2 Stratos. I have published[pdf] work I did on designing and implementing multi-tenant business process engine at ICWS 2011.

Get in touch with me on Twitter, Facebook or plain old-fashioned email.
